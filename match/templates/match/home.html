    About us

    This website was created for the class, CS 3240 at the University of Virginia. The purpose of the application is to find specific study partners with certain skills and majors. Users can create their own profile, list their skills and majors, and find others whom they would want to work with. The application's unique features include the ability to search for students with specific skills and majors, and a feature to save profiles they like.
